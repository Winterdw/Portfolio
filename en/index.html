About me

Quality Assurance (QA) with more than 3 years of practical experience in software testing, covering both automated and manual testing. Throughout my career, I have developed a strong expertise in creating test cases and implementing automation tools such as Cypress, applying agile methodologies and CI/CD to optimize processes and increase efficiency. My experience ranges from accessibility testing according to WCAG guidelines and Section 508 compliance to API testing, regression testing and performance testing, always with a focus on product quality and reliability. I am committed to identifying and solving critical problems before they reach the end user, ensuring that every delivery exceeds expectations.
